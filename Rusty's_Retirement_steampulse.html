Developer Action Items

  1. Add offline/closed-game progression — cited in ~30% of negative reviews as a core expectation violation for an idle game
  2. Allow farm orientation and layout changes post-creation — locked horizontal/vertical mode appears in ~25% of complaints across nearly every review batch
  3. Allow removal and rotation of placed buildings and animals — inability to delete or reposition is a top UX frustration appearing in ~25% of negative reviews
  4. Reduce early-game manual replanting burden — the 'not truly idle until Splunk unlock' complaint appears in nearly every batch (~30% of negatives); consider an earlier or cheaper basic auto-planter
  5. Reduce late-game achievement grind — the 250k harvest and fossil collection achievements are called out as excessively tedious in ~25% of reviews; add speedup mechanics or reduce thresholds
  6. Add meaningful content and mechanical differences between farm maps — 'later farms feel the same' appears in ~20% of reviews; unique crops, mechanics, or unlocks per map would help
  7. Expand endgame content — 'nothing to do after optimizing the farm' is a recurring complaint (~20% of reviews); add post-completion goals or a New Game+ mode
  8. Fix Linux transparency and Mac multi-monitor support — platform-breaking bugs appear across multiple batches and directly cause refunds from affected users
  9. Optimize CPU/GPU performance — high resource usage for an idle game is reported in ~15% of reviews and is antithetical to the background-play value proposition
  10. Add a basic tutorial or in-game help system — 'no tutorial' appears in ~15% of review batches; even a simple tooltip layer would reduce friction for new players
  11. Improve Splunk AI field-marking respect — bot ignoring player-designated fields is a concrete, fixable bug mentioned across multiple batches
  12. Add window position/size persistence between sessions — small but frequently mentioned QoL issue that undermines the desktop companion experience
