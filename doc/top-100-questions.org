* The 100 questions

These are example natural-language questions the platform should be able to
answer. Organized by persona and category. Each maps to structured queries
over our games, reviews, tags, genres, and reports data.

** Market Sizing & Trends (Developer / Analyst)
1. How many games were released on Steam in 2025 with more than 100 reviews?
2. What percentage of games released this year are tagged "Roguelike"?
3. Which genre had the highest growth in new releases year-over-year?
4. How many Early Access games graduated to 1.0 in the last 12 months?
5. What's the median review count for indie RPGs released in 2024?
6. How many free-to-play games crossed 1,000 reviews in their first year?
7. Which tags are gaining popularity fastest (most new games using them)?
8. What's the average time an Early Access game spends before 1.0 launch?
9. How many games priced above $30 achieved "Very Positive" or better?
10. What fraction of all Steam games have fewer than 10 reviews?

** Genre & Tag Intelligence
11. What are the top 10 most common tag combinations for games with 500+ reviews?
12. Which genres have the highest average sentiment scores?
13. What tags correlate most strongly with "Overwhelmingly Positive" sentiment?
14. Are "Souls-like" games trending up or down in review volume year-over-year?
15. Which genre has the widest spread between best and worst sentiment?
16. What's the average price point for "City Builder" games with 1000+ reviews?
17. How do survival games compare to crafting games in average playtime at review?
18. Which tags appear together most often in hidden gem games (high sentiment, low reviews)?
19. What genres have the highest ratio of negative to positive reviews?
20. Are incremental/idle games growing or shrinking as a category?

** Competitive & Audience Analysis
21. What games share the most reviewer overlap with Stardew Valley?
22. Which games are reviewed by the same players who review Factorio?
23. What's the audience overlap between "Vampire Survivors" and other action roguelikes?
24. For players who reviewed both Terraria and Starbound, which did they rate higher?
25. What are the top 5 games most frequently mentioned in Cyberpunk 2077 reviews?
26. Which competitors do reviewers of Hades mention most often?
27. What's the sentiment breakdown when players compare my game to a specific competitor?
28. Which developers have the most cross-game reviewer overlap with my studio?
29. For the "Metroidvania" tag, which 3 games share the most audience?
30. What game has the most unique audience (least overlap with other games)?

** Sentiment & Review Quality
31. What aspects are players most frustrated with in the RPG genre?
32. What are the most common complaints across all "Mostly Negative" games?
33. Which design elements are praised most often in "Overwhelmingly Positive" games?
34. What percentage of reviews for my game mention performance/crash issues?
35. Is the sentiment for No Man's Sky improving, stable, or declining over the last year?
36. Which games had the biggest sentiment reversal (negative to positive) after updates?
37. What are the top 5 most-requested features across all survival games?
38. Do games with controller support have higher average sentiment than those without?
39. What's the average sentiment difference between Early Access and post-launch reviews?
40. Which games have the highest proportion of "helpful" reviews (votes_helpful > 10)?

** Pricing & Revenue Intelligence
41. What's the sweet spot price point for indie roguelikes with 500+ reviews?
42. Do games priced at $19.99 perform better in sentiment than those at $29.99?
43. For the "Strategy" genre, what price range has the best review-to-price ratio?
44. Which free-to-play games have the worst monetization sentiment?
45. How does DLC sentiment compare to base game sentiment for games with DLC?
46. What's the average price of games tagged "Short" vs "Long" that are rated positively?
47. Are there games priced above $50 with "Very Positive" reviews? Which ones?
48. What percentage of negative reviews mention "not worth the price"?
49. Which price tier ($5, $10, $15, $20, $25, $30+) has the highest average sentiment?
50. Do games that launch at a discount have better or worse long-term sentiment?

** Developer Portfolio & Studio Analysis
51. Which indie studios have consistently "Very Positive" games across their catalog?
52. What's the average sentiment trajectory across a developer's second and third releases?
53. Which developers improved their review scores the most between their first and latest game?
54. For studios with 3+ games, what's their average sentiment variance?
55. Which publishers have the best track record for indie games (top 10 by avg sentiment)?
56. Do solo developers have higher or lower average sentiment than small studios?
57. Which developer has the most total reviews across all their games?
58. What percentage of a developer's audience carries over between sequels?
59. Which studios have the fastest review velocity in their first launch week?
60. Are there developers whose games consistently appear in "hidden gem" lists?

** Launch & Timing Strategy
61. What's the best month to launch an indie game based on historical review velocity?
62. Do games launched in January perform differently from games launched in September?
63. How many games launched in the same week as Elden Ring got fewer than 50 reviews?
64. What's the average first-week review count for games that eventually reach 1,000 reviews?
65. Is there a day-of-week effect on launch review velocity?
66. How do Steam Next Fest demo games perform in reviews compared to non-demo games?
67. What's the typical review velocity curve for a "successful" indie game in the first 30 days?
68. Do holiday releases (Nov-Dec) have higher or lower average sentiment?
69. What percentage of games that got fewer than 50 reviews in month one ever recovered to 500+?
70. Which release windows have the least competition for the "Roguelike" tag?

** Player Behavior & Engagement
71. What's the average playtime at review for "Overwhelmingly Positive" games vs "Mixed"?
72. At what playtime do most negative reviews get written for open-world games?
73. Is there a churn wall (common dropout point) for survival games?
74. Do players with 100+ hours write more positive reviews than those with <10 hours?
75. What's the median playtime difference between positive and negative reviewers for RPGs?
76. Which games have the longest average playtime at review?
77. What percentage of reviewers have fewer than 2 hours of playtime (potential refunders)?
78. For multiplayer games, how does playtime at review correlate with sentiment?
79. What's the average number of games a reviewer who reviewed my game also reviewed?
80. Do "funny" reviews (high votes_funny) skew positive or negative?

** Technical & Platform Analysis
81. Which games have the most crash/performance complaints as a percentage of reviews?
82. Do games with Linux support have different sentiment profiles than Windows-only?
83. What are the most common technical complaints across VR games?
84. Which game engines correlate with the most technical issue reports?
85. How does controller support affect sentiment for platformer games?
86. What percentage of negative reviews for AAA games mention DRM?
87. Which games have the biggest gap between Steam Deck verified and actual review sentiment?
88. Do games with workshop/mod support have higher sentiment than those without?
89. What are the most common save-related complaints across all games?
90. Which categories (multiplayer, co-op, cloud saves) correlate with higher sentiment?

** Content & Replayability
91. What games are most praised for replayability across all genres?
92. Which single-player games have the highest average playtime at review?
93. Do games with achievements have higher engagement (playtime) than those without?
94. What's the sentiment difference between "short but sweet" games (<5h) and 100+ hour games?
95. Which game has the best value-for-money perception based on review signals?

** Cross-Cutting & Big Picture
96. What are the top 10 hidden gems on Steam right now (high sentiment, low visibility)?
97. If I'm making a roguelike deck-builder, what are the top 5 complaints I should avoid?
98. What would a "State of Steam 2025" report look like — key stats and trends?
99. Which game released this year is most likely to become a long-tail evergreen seller?
100. Across all 6,000 analyzed games, what is the single most common player complaint?

* Data gaps (crawl-side, not architecture-side)
- No DLC↔base game relationship
- No game engine data
- No discount / price history
- No concurrent player counts
- No Steam Deck verified status (easy add — Steam API has it)
- No Steam Next Fest demo metadata
